Welcome to Sel

Here takes place the story of Elantris. It takes place shortly after the collapse of a once great society and details the struggles of life following that collapse.

Shards

There are two Shards that influence Sel: Devotion and Dominion.

Investiture

The type of magic used here is through the people's connection to the Dor, a type of investiture that exists in the cognitive realm but is able to be accessed by certain individuals on Sel through the use of Aons. Aons are runes or characters that these individuals can draw in the air in front of them. Draw the correct shape and a certain effect will be unleashed.
