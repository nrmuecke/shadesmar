Welcome to Roshar

This is the planet where the Stormlight Archives take place. A series of fantasy novels written by Sanderson detailing the events of a violent struggle between nations.

Shards

There are three Shards, otherwise known as Gods, that influence Roshar: Cultivation, Honor and Odium.

Investiture

The types of investiture or powers on Roshar include Fabrial Science, Surgebinding, Voidbinding, and the Old Magic, though Rosharans refer to it all as Surgebinding. Basically, they can harness stormlight and use it for certain powers such as flight, crafting illusions, or even healing.
